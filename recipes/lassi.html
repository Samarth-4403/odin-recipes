Lassi recipe

Lassi is a yogurt-based Indian drink that's creamy, refreshing, and comes in sweet or salty flavors. It's enjoyed during hot weather, complements spicy meals, and offers both taste and nutrition.

To make lassi, blend yogurt, water, sugar (for sweet lassi) or salt/spices (for salty lassi) until smooth. Optional additions include fruits or flavorings like rose or mango. Enjoy the creamy and refreshing lassi as a delightful beverage.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Water
  • Sugar or salt
  • Optional fruits or flavorings

Steps

  1. Blend yogurt, water, and desired flavorings together.
  2. If making sweet lassi, add sugar to taste. If making salty lassi, add salt or spices.
  3. Continue blending until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  4. Optional: Add fruits or additional flavorings for extra taste.
  5. Pour the lassi into glasses and serve chilled.
